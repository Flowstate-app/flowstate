# Privacy Policy for FlowState

Last updated: December 3, 2025

## Information We Collect
FlowState is a focus timer app that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data Storage
- All timer sessions, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- Purchases are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system

## Third-Party Services
- RevenueCat: Handles subscription management (privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- Apple In-App Purchases: Processes payments (privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
